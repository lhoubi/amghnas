About the Scripts

Preserving and celebrating Amazigh heritage through digital tools

ⵜⵉⴼⵉⵏⴰⵖ

Tifinagh Script

An ancient writing system used by Berber peoples for over 2,500 years, now standardized as Neo-Tifinagh by the IRCAM institute.

ⵜⴰⵎⴰⵣⵉⵖⵜ

Tamazight Language

The language of the Amazigh (Berber) people, spoken across North Africa from Morocco to Egypt. A rich oral and written tradition.

ⵜⴰⵍⴰⵜⵉⵏⵜ

Talatint Romanization

A Latin-based transcription system for Tamazight, using extended characters to represent sounds unique to the Amazigh language.
