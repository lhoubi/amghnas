Privacy Policy

Simple, honest, and respectful — just like our tools.

Last updated: March 2026
ⴰ

The short version

We don't collect, store, or sell your personal data. All text you type into our converter or keyboard stays on your device and is never sent to any server. amghnas is a static website — there is no database, no accounts, and no tracking of what you type.

This policy explains in more detail how amghnas.com handles information when you use our free tools.

ⴱ

What we collect

amghnas does not actively collect personal information. However, like any website, some basic technical data may be processed automatically:

  • Your browser type and version (used by your browser, not stored by us)
  • General location data (country level) via Google Analytics, if enabled
  • Pages visited and time spent (via Google Analytics, anonymized)
  • Search queries that led you to our site (via Google Search Console)

We use Google Analytics to understand how people find and use the site — this helps us improve the tools. You can opt out of Google Analytics using your browser's privacy settings or extensions like uBlock Origin.

ⵛ

Text you type into the tools

Your text never leaves your device. The Tifinagh converter, Latin converter, Arabic converter, and virtual keyboard all run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No text is sent to any server. We cannot see what you type.

Your theme preference (dark/light mode) is saved locally in your browser's localStorage — this never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

ⴷ

Cookies

amghnas uses minimal cookies:

  • Theme preference — stored in localStorage, never sent to a server
  • Google Analytics cookies — anonymous usage statistics (e.g. _ga, _gid). These help us understand which tools are most useful.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing tools.

ⴻ

Third-party services

amghnas uses the following external services:

  • Google Fonts — to load the Noto Sans Tifinagh, Cormorant Garamond, and DM Sans fonts. Google may log font requests.
  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic analysis. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. See GitHub's Privacy Policy.
ⴼ

Children's privacy

amghnas does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our tools are free educational resources suitable for all ages. We do not require registration or any personal information to use the site.

ⴳ

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of amghnas after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?

We're happy to answer any questions about how amghnas handles your data.
